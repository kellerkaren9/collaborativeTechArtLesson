Lesson Plan

Title Heritage Vessels
Designers Chrisitna Luby and Karen Keller
Summary Students are challenged to construct a vessel through the use of CAD programs that will symbolically represent aspects culture. Students will participate in group discussions focused on aspects of culture, ethnicity, heritage, and religion as it pertains to their identity.
Grade Level 5-8
Key Words
  • Culture- The set of shared attitudes, values, goals, practices, customary beliefs, social forms, and material traits of a racial, religious, or social group.
  • Ethnicity- a particular ethnic affiliation or group
  • Heritage- background from which one comes, or any sort of inherited property or goods
  • Religion- the belief in and worship of a superhuman controlling power, especially a personal God or gods.
  • Symbols- a mark or character used as a conventional representation of an object, function, or process, e.g., the letter or letters standing for a chemical element or a character in musical notation.
